Hmong New Year Festival

The Hmong celebrate the "Hmong New Year" to preserve community and culture New Year typically begins at the end of fall harvest to honor and thank our ancestors for a successful year.

Men and women don their intricate colorful outfits for an immersive gatering full of art, music, dance and food. The different colors and designs inform what dialect, region and a person might be from. It may also indicate what dialect you might speak.
